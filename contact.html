FAQ

Common Questions

Are these real deals or just estimates?
Every deal featured on this site is verified before posting. Prices are confirmed live from booking platforms. That said, fares can change within hours — always double-check before buying.
Do you book flights on my behalf?
No — I find and recommend the deals, then you book directly through the airline or booking platform. This keeps your payment information secure and ensures you have direct access to your booking.
What airports do you cover?
Primarily major Canadian airports: YYZ (Toronto), YVR (Vancouver), YUL (Montreal), YYC (Calgary), and YEG (Edmonton). I also cover select secondary airports for specific deals.
How do mistake fares work?
Airlines occasionally publish fares far below their intended price due to currency errors, system glitches, or manual entry mistakes. These can be ticketed and honoured — but they disappear within hours. Speed is everything.
Can you help with points and miles redemptions?
Absolutely. Aeroplan, Air Miles, and credit card travel programs are part of what I cover. The Full Trip Planning service includes a review of your points balance and the best ways to use them for your trip.
